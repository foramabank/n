Security

Your security, our priority

Your security, our priority At FORAMA BANK, we take the security of your personal information and transactions very seriously. We are committed to protecting your data with the most advanced security measures.

Our security measures:

Advanced encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted to ensure confidentiality.

Two-factor authentication (2FA): An extra layer of security to protect your account from unauthorized access.

Real-time monitoring: We continuously monitor our systems to detect and prevent any suspicious activity.

Fraud protection: Our sophisticated fraud detection systems identify and block fraudulent transactions before they affect your account.

Ongoing training: Our employees receive regular training to stay up to date on best security practices.

Tips to protect your account: Never share your login information with anyone.

Use strong passwords and change them regularly.

Be cautious of suspicious emails and messages requesting personal information.

For any questions regarding the security of your account, our client support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
